About me

Former method intructor in social work trying out new waters in the world of web development through my education as web developer in e-commerce at Medieinstitutet.

The studying of web development reconcile my big interest of computers from my youth with a professional career. My experience from leading smaller projects and quality assurance in social work gives me a bigger understanding of working in a team-based industry.

As a person I am social and interested in others. This brought me to the studies of psychology where I spent more than three years asking, “Why people act as they do” and “How can a person/group change the way they act”. These questions are of course very hard to answer. My way to approach these questions were through working in a research group that studied causes of anti-social violence with a neuro-psychological approach.

Born and living in Gothenburg I enjoy the closeness to the sea and the opportunities a bigger city offers when it comes to concerts and other cultural activities. One of my other passions in life is martial arts in general and Brazilian jiu-jitsu in particular witch I have been teaching and training for 10+ years.
